My Story

My journey as a Data Engineer started with a fascination for how data shapes decisions in today’s world. With over four years of hands-on experience, I have worked on designing and optimizing data pipelines to transform raw information into valuable insights. My expertise spans across advanced technologies like AWS (AWS Glue, S3, and Athena), Snowflake, and big data platforms such as Hadoop and Spark.

I specialize in making data accessible, secure, and actionable for businesses by automating workflows and streamlining processes. My work is driven by a commitment to precision and a deep understanding of each organization’s unique needs, enabling me to build scalable, high-performance data solutions that support effective decision-making.

What I enjoy most about data engineering is the challenge of balancing technical and business objectives. I believe that data solutions are only as powerful as their usability, and this philosophy guides my approach in every project. I thrive in collaborative settings, working closely with cross-functional teams to deliver impactful insights that empower stakeholders and support growth.
